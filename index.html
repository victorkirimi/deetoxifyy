Kenya support contacts: NACADA (National Authority for the Campaign Against Alcohol and Drug Abuse) runs a free 24/7 helpline — dial 1192 from any Safaricom or Telkom line for telephone counseling and referrals to an accredited center, or email help@nacada.go.ke. Kenya Red Cross also runs a toll-free line on 1199. A few NACADA-accredited treatment centers: Nairobi Place Addiction Treatment Centre, Nairobi — 020 267 9839 / 0735 550 000; Comfort Care 360, Lavington, Nairobi — 0700 705 487; Fountain of Hope Rehab, Syokimau — 0796 656 300. Centers, numbers, and accreditation status change, so confirm details against NACADA's current list at nacada.go.ke before publishing or relying on them. Outside Kenya, search "crisis helpline" plus your country. This tool is a tracking aid, not a substitute for medical or professional support — withdrawal from some substances can be medically serious, so please involve a doctor or accredited treatment provider for the detox process itself.
